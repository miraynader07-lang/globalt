Description

Now that smartphones are in everyone’s hands, many services have shifted to electronic platforms to make it easier for users to access medical services. One of the most important of these services is obtaining medications. This service aims to provide you with access to any medication or medical product quickly and easily by delivering it directly to the patient’s home at an affordable cost, depending on the patient’s ability

Services

1. Providing rare or missing medications in pharmacies or local markets, with guarantees.
2. Ensuring that all medications offered by the program are verified through a special checking and monitoring system.
3. Sending alerts to patients when medications they previously searched for become available.
4. Delivering medications to patients’ homes quickly with guarantees that the medication reaches the correct person.
5. Delivery through the Egyptian postal service and partnerships with government agencies and private companies to ensure fast delivery.
6. Full reliance on technology to manage and deliver medications to homes.

Target

1. Pharmacies and hospitals: to purchase a wide range of medications.
2. Doctors: who need specific medications for certain conditions.
3. Pharmaceutical companies: that need medications to distribute or use.
4. Individuals: who are looking for rare or unavailable medications locally.
5. Government agencies and healthcare organizations: to provide medications for patient care.
6. Brokers or clients: buying medications from abroad.
